What is Vyna.live Assistant?

Vyna.live Assistant is a powerful browser extension designed to help streamers and content creators research, take notes, and generate content ideas while browsing the web.

AI Chat

Ask questions about your research, get content ideas, and brainstorm with our AI assistant directly in your browser.

Smart Notes

Save and organize your research findings, content ideas, and citations with just a few clicks.

Web Integration

Extract content from any web page and use it in your research or notes.

Getting Started

To start using Vyna.live Assistant:

  1. Click on the Vyna.live icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Sign in with your Vyna.live account (or create a new one)
  3. Start using the AI assistant or taking notes while you browse
